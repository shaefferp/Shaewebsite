So... What does Earth Tech Resources PBC do?

We assist developers in areas designated as underserved and distressed communities. This encourages investors to develop these areas and provide new jobs, economic growth, and stimulates expansion in the communities being served.

We are a Public Benefits Corporation registered in Texas and our market areas incude primarily Texas and Oklahoma at this time. In addition to serving distressed communities our goal is to invest our profits to provide growth in artistic, charitable, cultural, economic, literary, medical, religious, scientific, or technological areas in underserved communities to create a better quality of life. We will focus on assisting veterans and many others that are in need of aid.

We are also the home of the trademarked "SMART Park" , which stands for Sustainable Manufacturibg Agricultural Renewable Technology Park. Think conceptually of combining agriculture, manufacturing and recycling together with the goal of maximum efficiency and minimal or no waste. Add in the Eco friendly renewable and alternative power sources supplied by wind, biofuels, solar or possibly even hydrogen power. Next realize that you have created an a sustainable project managed and funded through the creation of an agricultural district district to house all this. (ask us how)

You now have a profitable business unit, with zero waste, great profits, tax revenue, and possibly "Tax Credits" and "Renewable Energy Credits" from the government. You also now have created jobs, and are providing quality products to your local community, within 200 miles. Wow? It's a big deal right?
